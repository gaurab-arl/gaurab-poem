Happy

Happy person I see — The childish innocence, Wanting to help others in need. A person with beauty and brain, Yet the feeling of hopelessness — She feels. I can't resolve it. The person who sits beside me Is empty. From the back I see — The friend I made Back then Is leaving. The short-term interest Shown in me Was a curiosity. The dream Of being the one All the way to the end — Is scattering. The friend I wanted to be — I was never depressed, But I feel Emptiness in me. The void in the sky I see — It reflects a mirror in me, Always presenting The person Others wanted me to be. I was never my true self, Because Others never tried To see it through me. I am the same as others, But the difference Is in thinking — The person They share their thoughts to. But for me… it’s just me. I wanted to other What I feel But its me overthinking. What do I say to myself? All it says is: “Just leave it. You were never capable Of doing anything.” I wanted to cry that day. The tears rose in my eyes, But I stopped. Then— After some time, It started to rain. I tried many times, But no one saw me. How do I tell Of the suffering I’m feeling? I don’t know how to put It into words I know. Please, try to understand. Please— Hug me The moment my eyes Refuse to dry. I tried to feel others’ pain And talk to them… But they felt It was mocking. They showed their goodwill, They listened… Then they left me. A feeling Of being lost Grew inside me. The person I wanted to be, For them to see, Feels like a curse To me. How to become a person? Someone— Please, Teach me.
