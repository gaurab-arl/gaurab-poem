Dyed in Your Color

In a daydream, I’m a glass full of water. You dropped ink of love, I change color— Dyed in your color, losing my own. You vanish, but I remain, Forever stained by you alone. My mind seems to kill me, Thinking of you, I’m lost in a sea. Like a fountain overflowing with emotion. I seem buried, deep down within, Still echoing the version I had been.
- Gaurab Aryal
