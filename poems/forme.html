Once I left, Then came back once again. The feeling I left behind — I don't wanna see. Now as I know, And others see, They ask about me... But — I didn’t say a thing. I Passed it to the next person I see. But — Now it’s my turn again, And I still won't say a thing. The truth inside, I had scraped it out. Now I see — Pain whispers to me: “Do you want to be lonely?” The feeling of being left out Keeps growing inside me, Drowning in a pool Of wild imagination. The song I listened to was depressing, Forcing me To think too much about myself. The loneliness I felt, Told me to vanish, Quietly, Without saying anything. Lost in thoughts all night, I can’t sleep. But it’s you I’m thankful for — For everything. I felt happy. Putting the loneliness aside, You gave me A sense of importance. You kept reminding me: “Don’t sink too deeply.” You tried pulling me up. I didn’t understand — Was it a test? For me. Keeping me up late at night So I’d fall deeper. But it wasn’t love Or friendship — It was humanity, You said to me. I flowed with the current You gave me. Told you what I felt. This isn’t a love story. It’s just pure friendship — And curiosity. I waited to talk. And maybe... Maybe I won’t be lost next time, If I see and feel again. Seeing you Was like walking into A garden full of beautiful flowers. And I — I was the unique thorny one In the middle. The words I heard Keep echoing: “Was I a good person?” You kept saying I was. Maybe you're just too good That you see good in others. But the dark side I hide — It’s not dark. It’s the darkest. So don’t come near me. Don't look at the fear in me. It’s growing. Still growing. Maybe it's my mistake — The one that keeps pulling me back. This insecurity of lagging behind Is all I ever see. The sweet, funny words You said to me — Maybe it’s just me, Distancing. The long nights Felt short while talking. But my sleepy mind Keeps dimming. I just want to improve. To not be left out — That’s all I feel. Let me tell you something: You are the first person I’ve ever written something deep for. You — Hardworking, Funny, The brightest person I see. Maybe you feel the same too. A depressing feeling, Ringing through. Maybe you cried once, With no one to lean on. I felt that too. And maybe — That feeling left you After talking to someone You trusted. You block the view from others. They see something — But I still don’t understand What you really feel About me. Is it friendship That I see? Or is it just Humanity That you feel?
