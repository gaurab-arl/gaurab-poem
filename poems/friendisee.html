Friend I See

A letter to a once-cherished friend, now strained by jealousy, misunderstanding, and silent heartbreak

O Friend, I failed to see it — Always showing different things, I couldn't tell anything. You look like a selfish person, Wanting to keep everything But giving nothing back. You changed Because you told me something... Were you disturbed Because I acted like I didn't listen? The girl you loved Didn't give anything in return — Now, I talk to her. Are you jealous? Now things have gone North and south. Now you doubt me, And try to probe me. But that's too bad — I learned those things When you were still playing. The hope you gave me... I am still thinking: How we got there, Where I was never meant to be. Is that jealousy and anger You are feeling? Trying different things You were not supposed to — Is that the likeness That she had seen in me? Still thinking — Why did we get stuck On something That only poisons us slowly? Wanting to test me — It's not easy. Trying to break things Even before starting them. I am still thinking: Why not wanting to see me? Or help me? Is that because of jealousy? Is it because of her? Or am I improving? Why have you become rude to me? Wasn't the previous breakup A lesson for you? Or is that excitement you feel — That if you can't have something, I shouldn't have it either? Why disturb her While talking to me? Why act weirdly? Is it attention you seek? I hear bad words About the dear one I found — Where were you at that time? You were given A special position — Before me. Why is she always on your side? I think... She has already found Her special person, While I was nothing more Than a temporary friend. Lost in thought — Maybe thinking about her — Why try for something That already belongs to someone else? Even if you approach her, Leave it — She is different. My friend, Let me tell you: The girl you are seeing Has another "me" in her heart. How lucky for me — It is the same feeling, But not actually me.
- Gaurab Aryal
