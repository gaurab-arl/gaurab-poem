Deature

"Departure" captures the silent heartbreak of loving someone from afar, knowing you will never reach them.

I see you, don't know if I know you. Today a friend asked me a thing I don't want to tell him. Maybe he guessed it right, but what's bad luck — he faced, I didn't tell him. My friend said to me, "Who was the lucky girl?" I answered with a heavy heart, "The lucky girl I see is never meant to be with me." I will just continue to see from a far distance where she can't see me. Maybe I will be in heaven or in hell. The person I see is growing old with time passing — it's also me, I feel. Wrinkles — or something I love to see. Maybe we will be together after we complete our living will. She has a person, I know it too. Maybe that's why I don't approach you. The missingness I feel looking at you blows me into pieces. Lost in lists I don't see, lost in thoughts of me I don't think, lost in caring for you — you didn't feel. The burning eyes of others I see speak to me, telling me to make distance from her. The different species, same feeling I see, but why does it have to be me? If I didn't talk, don't forget — I didn't hug, didn't cry. If I didn't see, don't search for me. A moment of silence is my friend. Frustration is all I see, anxiety is what I feel, depression is all I get. Deep in abyss I feel — maybe it's a place I don't ever want you to see. Imagined a day you left me, imagined a day you misunderstood me — I will leave in anger I feel, will be left in all conversations you involved in. It's the dead end for me. Always lost in thoughts, I will never overcome it. Now divided into two: one side dark, and one side white, with the color reflected toward me — far from you. Things I said are only limited to me, as I will not share with anybody. Things I heard and see — many things are different than I expected them to be. I least expected to be asked about it, but that was all a dream, started by a gentle wind. Well, good morning — it's morning. Everything you do turns out to be something beautiful. Everything I do turns out to be something painful. Then I took my turn to do the things I have done — which were meaningful. You know I love you. If you jump into fire, I will jump too just to get you. Then I know — you were on the opposite side where flowers were full grown. I was in fire where I stood alone.
- Gaurab Aryal
