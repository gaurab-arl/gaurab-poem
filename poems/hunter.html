The Hunter Goodbye

I am a hunter , You are my prey Even if I love , I can't say It's hurts too much, I won't be sane I learned to observe you far away Now I can’t make you stay. I’d choose you before myself each day, But look at me, I haven’t faded away. Waking up in morning to find you In a dark it's like searching a pearl You’re beautiful, as you are, my dear But I’m a hunter, caught as prey right here. I’m bleeding out, you’re free to run again Just fly away to horizon where no one stay In empty spaces, we sare our names, We look eye to eye from far away , A goodbye Can't even communicate, But laughing And in silence, we both walk our own way.
- Gaurab Aryal
