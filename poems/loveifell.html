Love

A confession of regrets, unfulfilled dreams, and a deep wish to protect and uplift, even from the shadows.

O love I feel— I failed to feel, Failed to accept it. Was it something possible for me to feel? The wishes you had, Please tell me about them. I may not be able to fulfill all of them, But I will try — I will be able to do it. Please forgive me, The anger I got Nearly destroyed me. Please forgive me The weakness I had in me Is that I attached to things very deeply. When I shouted at you, I felt betrayed by myself — A lone figure on tall mountains, Still waiting for me. Everything is empty around you, But filled with memories of me. Things you have done for me — I will never, ever forget them. Sometimes, The anger got me in every way possible. Broken down into particles, I couldn't combine it. The anger I felt — I couldn't do a thing. I also wanted you to be happy, But all the fault is on me. I couldn't understand your feelings. You just wanted to help me too, Maybe you saw your dreams through me. Please forgive me — I couldn't look into you. I know, You had a rough life because of me. Was I the biggest happiness You ever got in your whole journey? Please forgive me For the dream I couldn't fulfill, For the hope that died within me, For the pain you have been suffering. When I think of you, I only respect you. But when I think around you, I get lost. Lost every hope — Loose like tears, Flowing like a river. Couldn't get up like I used to; Now my leg is broken. You are pure as the sun — I see you in every possible way. You always light me up For something I never expected. Love, why are you growing Even when I don't deserve it? Please leave me — Other than crying, I won't do anything. I am infected by outer things — I may never recover. Lost in you, Never meant to doubt that. It's just that I didn't try enough. The dream you dreamed of — Please let me fulfill it. For me, You are in depth and debt So deep I couldn't even imagine. But it's also me — Realized too late, Sank too deeply. I am also in it. Let me sacrifice myself for your bright future. Let me live in darkness, So that — I will always push you in the right path.
- Gaurab Aryal
