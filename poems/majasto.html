Ma Jasto

Mero mutu lageu timile, malai darkauna na deu. Timi jata chhau, malai herna deu. Timro khushi ma malai ramauna deu. Timro saath na huda pani, "Timro saathi ho," bhan na deu. Sajhako bela haami hunthiyau bhet— bokera aafno mann ma pida Heriraheko thiyau— ek arkā lai buj theu eak arko ko aakha bata kura, ruun thieam bujhera ek arkā ko pida. Sundar timro muhar, hira jastai timra aakha— mandirma rakheko murti jasto: jati herda ni heri rahun kasto. Sanga haali rakheko chu— mann ma dherai kura. Kasari bhanu timilai sāra? Bujdainau bhanchau timi— khoi, kasto kasto? Dherai kura lukaune bhaeko chhau, ma jasto.
- Gaurab Aryal
