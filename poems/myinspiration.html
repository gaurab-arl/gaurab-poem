Night with My Inspiration

Filled with belonging, with my story She fell like a dream yet to be fulfilled. Full of love, of cuteness, of laughter and light A girl who came to soothe my silent ache with care. On a journey we walked; I wished to carry her weight. Her shiny face, those long lashes, her curly hair So serious at times, yet she'd mock my foolish ways. A playful date, a genuine smile lighting her face. We talked without worry, as if time had paused. At a crossroad, she pulled me back from behind. Afraid of losing her, I held on tight Together we crossed, yet the end felt close. In the end, I left— fearing my inner self. Hesitation filled my heart where she resides. She was a chapter that slipped away, With a call, I assured myself—the hesitation that killed me.
- Gaurab Aryal
