Girl I See

Far From a Distance" is a heartfelt journey

A girl I see Looks like a women that raised me, Have something , I can't tell it. Far from a distance, I see you. Looking at you, I feel happy. You look like a fairy — The one I always dreamed of, The one I always felt in my heart. Tell me — Do you dream of me? As a hero saving you, Or as a child searching for you? I see your happy family — Truly, the dream girl I always wished for. Can you be mine, Even if you don’t know me? Can you be mine, Even if I never ask for? The hug I imagine is empty — Without you. The warmth I wish for Feels cold — Without you. What’s a happy moment If I can't see you? What’s left in me If I don't have you? That blue dress you wore — Maybe thats the wife , I See, Standing silently beside me. That was a joke, Please forget about it. I don’t know what more to say, But everything about you Matches inside me. A person longing To be the child of the woman he loves — Maybe that's who I am. I have accepted it. Now, Maybe it’s your turn to accept me. Obsessed with your arrival, You changed me Beyond recognition. A hug is all I need. Please, Give me it to me. I will be happy forever If you just talk to me. Lost in thoughts of you, Still hoping — Please accept me. When you ask me what girl I looked, It feels like an avalanche: Slipping slowly... Until I fade away. Can you feel my emotions? Can you hold my hand? A face so beautiful, Never seen a fault. A person so imaginary, Yet feels so real. Is this what they call love? Or just pure affection? I don’t know. It’s confusing. I once listened to something called loved twice: First, the attraction to beauty and scent, Second, the understanding of habits and heart. Which one will I define? It’s the second. I saw the real you — Hidden behind the mask, An imperfect girl, Distancing herself from others, Waiting for someone to see. The heart you guarded for so long Has loosened, By the breeze I sent your way, this is a dream i see about her. I see you — Though you don’t know If I even exist. In my eyes, In my thoughts, It was only you. The girl , I see is growing older. So , Do I I see wrinkles, I see passing years, And I still love. Lost in dreams That did’t exist. Lost in thoughts That never spoke. Lost in caring You never felt. The burning eyes I see — The unfamiliar feelings — The unknown dreams. The dreams I see Whisper to me: Is this the truth? Unknown faces, Unknown eyes, I find. You didn’t talk — I didn’t forget, You didn’t see — I didn’t care You didn’t look happy, I stayed silent . Hiding the tears I never cried. The moon feels cold. Understand my feelings.
- Gaurab Aryal
