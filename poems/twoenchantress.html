TWO DEMON

Two demons — one white, the other black as haze, Eyes like abyss, staring deep into my maze. One seemed holy, with a complex, tempting grace, I was a slave beneath their burning gaze. Sweet as honey, bitter as poison, What I drank gave unbearable pain. Two sides of a cursed coin - one gave hope, The other was death, no escape. I kept walking, still under their stare, Like flickering lights, they vanished into air. Playful as children, mature as queens, Their interest flickered — water rippling in streams. I was just an expense in their cruel game, Use me , then throw me away.
- Gaurab Aryal
