Illusion

A journey through the realms of imagination and dreams, where reality blends with fantasy.

An illusion— a state where dreams come true, where I see a loved one, see death, see magic and see peace. A world of wonders— where I want to live and die. Free from shackles and pain, where I want to fly. I want to go there alone, with the humming melody following me— a world, a place, where I always wanted to be. A world full of imagination— who doesn't want that? I did. But now I see all the reality, as it keeps pulling me, to a darker side where I never wanted to be, buried with shadows , where i couldn’t see. Others push me as I go deeper, and reality keeps hitting me like a checkerboard of pain, as I try to find the surface where I once flew free. An illusion keeps checking on me. The girl I saw yesterday was a little girl grown in a day— maybe tomorrow I'll see her as a child. But how can I show her my feelings if I never wanted her? Yet I long for her. The place we used to talk, walk, where we whispered sweet things— now filled with fog. The distance between us has grown so wide. From afar, I used to see you wave… now you’ve disappeared. The closer I felt, the more you disappeard. The illusion kept me blind. The more I reached for closure, the more distant you became. The closer I got, the farther you traveled. The waving that once belonged to me— now directed toward a stranger. Like a river flooded with water, I was scattered. My eyes filled with tear more i lost . Now, I see the dead and a place i wanted to be, to see that person— whether dream or illusion, I just want to see. All i wanted was to see, Was a butterfly with you, Which my friends used to say. Now you left with another, for a better opportunity. Fly like a beautiful butterfly— all eyes on me. I want to die, quickly and quietly, as everyone remembers what I wanted to be. The more pain I endured in life, the happiest where the person, I dont want to see. The distance you created, has grown too wide, where i cant reach. Where I once saw sunshine, I now see blacklight. The dark fire inside me has grown wild. And yet, when I look at the sky, I feel warmer— because maybe, that’s where I’ll die. This is a phase. I see what i wanted to see, I wrote about a girl who has grown big but the thing I held for her has vanished in illusion where my last resting place used to be.
- Gaurab Aryal
