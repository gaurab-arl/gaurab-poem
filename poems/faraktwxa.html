Farak Ta Cha

Farak ta cha, Yo lekhne manxe ma, Jasle saas feri raheko cha. bado umer mero, kunai samaye sano ballak thiye, aaba mitru najikidai xa. auta khatha mero ,kunai yatra ko Mukhya Naam maile birseye ko Bihana uthne chara ma, Uu thi kiran gham ko. Farak ta cha, timi thiyo bihana ko gadi chadna buleko yatri ma, timi mero pugna parni thau gadi ma nidaye ko manxe ma. gadi xode ra, hinda hindai kura haru ma aljiyeko ma timi lekhni kabito ko sirsak navayeni pani, auta patra ho ma. Farak ta cha, Plane herni aakashma, sano ballak ma, Plane chadi udhni balika timi. Herxau baira plane ko jhyaalko mathi bata nadehkhi, Ek manxe ma. Farak ta cha, Kitab ko ek dui sabda ma, Sabda kosh timi, pustakalaya ko ratna. Farak ta cha, kira ho ma, timlai nachahane pida ho ma, baseko xu manxe haru ko ghera maa ma timle khojda khechiye ko Auta sano kira ho ma … Farak ta cha, Sapana ma dulhani pagal ho ma, Timi doctor, jachaune manxe. Bato ma rundai basni, Pidit Birami ho ma.
- Gaurab Aryal
