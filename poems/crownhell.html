Crown of Hell

I knew the sweetness of exhaustion, This broken body of clay and mud. A heart unable to hold the flood A rainbow painted only in red and black. With every whisper floating past, I stayed in the center, not listening. A temporary barrier, squeezed inside, Filled with blood, with only a trace of hope. Stay forever, staring at me: The devil, the summoner, the dead body. Peeking at the secrets hidden in the dark, Offering the crown of hell, Submit to me.
- Gaurab Aryal
