A Goddess and Her Apostle

Someone with a half-open door inside, Waiting for someone to arrive. Searching for a butterfly — One that never stays in sight. The fear of losing hides within, But can't be shown around love. Hope for support fills the mind, Yet that someone was lost — To another, kind and kind. Filled with the aroma of expectation, A ritual of sacred love in silent connection. Untold stories, heavy with pain, A friendship that breathes through hidden strain. The nature of six paths, the blend of two, Formed something unreal — In a small, quiet room. A sacred place, where nature flows, And emotions bloom in those who know.
- Gaurab Aryal
