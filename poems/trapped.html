Trapped in the Web

Sorry… the word I hear. I brought flowers, dripping with my blood— scars all over me. The devil smiles with me, thinking he's met someone just like him. The mixed hints that kept me locked in a box of beautiful dreams— dreams of you and me. A shattered soul, a shattered dream, for the one I wanted to live with. The dying son of a mother sees a blurry sky, eyes veiled with water. Crystal-like gems fall— wasted on something that never felt like his. An insect trapped in a spider's web… Should he jump from there— to another web of another spider? The hungry faces of spiders look at me like I'm a delicious meal, craving me endlessly— yet never eating. The fear keeps growing. I seem lost— lost to the fear, to the thoughts trapped inside me.
- Gaurab Aryal
