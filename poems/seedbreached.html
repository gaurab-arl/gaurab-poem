A Seed Breached

The seed that I once breached Has grown into the tree, Promising shelter for me, Still, I fed it, chasing my dream. The more I feed, the more it breathed The shelter that crushed me beneath. Under the weight of that tree, I become meat paste , from that pressed me. The more I think, the more I lose, An end draws near, no path to choose. Unable to bear the seed I sowed I left my soul — for next time, may it grow big.
- Gaurab Aryal
