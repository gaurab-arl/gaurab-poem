Terror of Person

Even the kindest soul was lost to him, Demons circled, deeply obsessed. Embodiment of darkness he was, A gleam of terror in every breath. Every lie wrapped in a smile, Confidence that fooled like a child, Full of himself, yet never bright, With no beginning and no end. Just a third wheel, a plague flea, No shame, no gain, no game, A superior thought, seeming like God, A mere human with shattered nerves.
- Gaurab Aryal
