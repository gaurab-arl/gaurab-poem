Tao of Two People

Bright and Dark path———— A disturbing sight Created waves unknown. When they struck something emotionless, A spark ignited, And fire came to life. Something hidden from all — Then I came to see. At first unsure, Maybe I just never noticed. But slowly, the veil was removed. My eyes lit up — And I realized: It was a beautiful story. It held a word Not found in my dictionary. A path familiar, But never quite aligned. I kept seeking that spark, Longing to see What always struck me — Without knowing why. Someone looked around… Saw something no one else tried to. A shadow by side — Like the Tao. One side was bright, The other, dark within. What bound them? Not love — But the hum of bees. A binding light between two souls, Two crescent moons combined To form a full one. Yet still — a hole in the middle. And to fulfill it… It needs a child — Carrying new life.
- Gaurab Aryal
