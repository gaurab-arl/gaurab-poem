THINKING

thinking about something endless

Fear resides inside me When I am thinking. What if someone is reading The thoughts I am thinking? Where is the god I serve? Where is He? I am searching. The god I wish to meet, The god who forsakes me— Where is the god I serve? The god I thought didn’t exist? The mask I once wore Is cracking. The things I was hiding Are viewing The one who forsook me— Is watching. Is it a child or a demon That I’m hiding? From the outside, I seem mature, With a sharp mind. But inside, I’m just a child, Lost in a dream— The dream I have been dreaming.
- Gaurab Aryal
