A Friend for a Moment, a Stranger the Next

A friend for a moment, a stranger in the next, Just behind my back, a sound keeps echoing. I listen— Yet I don't seem to care. A hidden story, like a river, Flowing only to draw near. Near me, the land shifted— And I slipped away. I jumped into the river, It wouldn't let me see my face, As fear pulled me under. A mirror—it keeps staring at me, Like a fly that won't fly away. It seems to like me quite a lot, Even though it's just a fish That slips away. "I try. I strive... But how can I hope, When even gods cannot survive?" The survival game we play— With faces that change like tides. Change the person, And I change too. A new self for everyone. Just choose and select the interface I lost me , In caring too much For everyone else.
- Gaurab Aryal
