Flying

Flying high in the sky, Floating, Looking at you from the cloudy side. It rained on me— The feeling of being seen, From the beginning, you noticed me. I seem to disappear from time to time, Yet you were searching. You seemed like an angry bird, White and red in smile— A playful girl, Always wanting to be different From the regular crowd. A story of Husband, wife, and daughter, Sitting together Beside me. I was listening To their feelings. A happy couple Travelling, I was just a guy waiting. A round circle— I’ve been walking. What’s the end? It seems like The start, where I began walking. Well, thanks For clearing my mind. I misunderstood All things. Once rejected can also be Accepted Well tell me is it My overthinking Or lets paly truth and dare. A love story Started when I was away The time you spend together Tell me some of them. A sentence you always said— Now just kept it Safe in your place. I was just in sky You were traveling I was thinking You were loving.
- Gaurab Aryal
