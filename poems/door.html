A closed Door To Heart

A door to her heart — When I asked her to put me in, She said it's closed. Waiting was the only option, Even if it ends in failure. Maybe I will end up Becoming like a skeleton, Or in heaven where a fairy glows. Hey, A fairy from heaven — I am growing old. Just look at me At your front door. A door towards your heart, Where I can't go. The restrictions you made — Is it temporary or permanent? Or will we see as time goes? Waiting for the door to open Again, When you closed . Maybe you will not Recognize me after 5–10 years old. I think the story will be The only thing remaining Between me and your. The labor of waiting forever — Is it the pain you said? Or is it you That I get? Does a wound still ache from The past You tried to forget? Or is it the Fear of not being qualified for? Or something you can't tell ? I see you as complete, With some parts missing — Just searching For repairment, Maybe by me or someone else. Something was lost in me — I don't know what you stole, But it's all right, As it was never mine, Its truly yours. As I wait for you, Will the door always be closed? It seems I am not the person Who exists in your dreams, I am a seller, Lost in sea. You didn’t find yourself in me As I thought. You are lost To a broken bridge Where no person goes. At the beginning or end, Just see — maybe I am there, Drowning in sorrow. A feeling at the beginning — Was it lost or suppressed By your thoughts? Is it due to not accepting the facts, Or did you try to distance yourself from them? A heart seems heavy With heavy thoughts. Even if something was lost, It's time for new things to come. As I try to understand, Please be open. Even if it’s closed, I will continue to search. Maybe it will end up Being my experience, Or achieving my goal. Even if it’s something — Why suppress it? Let it come. Maybe not this time, But after some time — Let it be a storm. Just take me with you When you come.
- Gaurab Aryal
