Sunlight

Seeing each others?

Phase of Seeing I was the candle, You were the night. I burned for you— Yet you longed for sunlight. The dream I have been dreaming Will be fulfilled by someone Unknown to me. The absence I faced, The weight I wear, Is suffocating the heart inside me. Where are you? I am searching. The pain you have given me Is surfacing. The night I have been dreaming, The hand I once held— Is leaving. The future I am seeing Is darkening. The sunlight you are going toward Is burning. The endless stories I once listened to— Are ending. The story where I once was Is scattering. The smile I see by my side Is disappearing. The tears I once hidden Are coming. The love I gave to her Is longing. It feels like a god's blessing— That I can’t see you Anymore than this. The comfort I give Is the warm feeling, Like a sunset I am setting. What’s the stage we are in? I think it is the phase Of seeing.
- Gaurab Aryal
