Vibration with Melody

Melody flowing with colors through me A sadness, happiness, envy, fear— Each contains profound meanings, But I can't seem to catch any of the feelings. With four of us locking eyes on me, Awkwardness filled my empty feelings. A pinch of time passed without knowing, All seemed to hover from place toward exit. A door with no exit, all eyes on me, Holding a key in my hand, looking in front of me. I seemed like a scarecrow—a blink on me, Going through a phase, with empty feeling.
- Gaurab Aryal
