The Soul, Ignited by Water

Cycle Every day, embrace a new lesson— new to learn, old mistakes in repetition. The loneliness, part of the cycle, diving deeper… deeper. A single soul, ignited by water, a gale of fire, yet void of desire Why see poison in everything? Just drink—think of it as portion. I saw the eyes of dying men, one looked at me and smiled, thinking:"I was just like you once— now, I leave it all behind." The past is dead, just like me. Living today, to die tonight. Just drink hot water, stay still— tomorrow, everything will be fine.
- Gaurab Aryal
