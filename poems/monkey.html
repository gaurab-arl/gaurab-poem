Hanging Monkey

Hanging like a monkey on the bus— Left, right, front, back, Like a boat on the sea, unstable. Missing the comfortable feeling of sitting on a seat. Looking forward to sitting, But as time passes, the feeling disappears. Hope continues to fade as the destination closes, Until the last moment I got off the bus—no seat, no peace, just the end.
- Gaurab Aryal
