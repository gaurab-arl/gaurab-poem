Nowhere to Stay

The drive once lost in time, The person we sought is no more. The inspiration we thought never existed, The one you see has disguised himself. Trying to survive in strange environments, You can’t see yourself molding, Forced to adapt in conditions, Where sparks fade, with nowhere to go. No matter what you do, what you think, What you want feels superficial. Clarity comes only with time, Revealing a place you don’t belong. Becoming someone, yet belonging nowhere. A reckless wind trapped in a confined space, Moving through places where no one breathes, Corner to corner, Yet never finding anywhere to stay.
- Gaurab Aryal
