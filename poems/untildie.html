Until I Die I Care

I am bitten to death, A happy end I see as dead end. I used to love, but I am burning alive, Till my eyes dry, I cried. A friend of mine by side, Keeping in arm, holding a piece of candle, Lighting a cigarette and enjoying The beautiful moth burning scene. Till the end, let me love you until I die, And with bruises all over me, Hanging on ceiling with the gift wrapper Gifted by my friend.
- Gaurab Aryal
