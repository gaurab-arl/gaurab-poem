Mental Age Thirty-Two

Old lady vibes, age thirty-two mentally, Nowadays it’s up, maybe forty-eight. Hidden like candies, looks like a child, Time for retirement? Nah, still wild. Alone in room thinking a game Tea cup in hand, rolls eyes gently. Laughs at chaos, but keeps it in line, “Too old for drama,” yet younger than time. A model or blonde? No one can say, Remembers her old self, a scary dare day. Counting candles but forgetting the count, A treasure of secrets, never spells them out.
- Gaurab Aryal
