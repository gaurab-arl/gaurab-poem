Spilling Secrets Like Water

Coming from someone you can look up to— Maybe it’s just my height. If I were shorter, you’d look me in the eye, If I were tiny, you’d see me every time. A secret slipped , just an accident, Like telling me your name, When everyone else already knows who you are, Guess it was a good day all the same. Maybe ,You keep spilling secrets like water— Overflowing, My hands can’t catch, I searched buckets to hold, until it’s dried, I just laugh, waiting for the next splash.
- Gaurab Aryal
