Better Not to Know

Not everything can be seen, Not everything can be peeled, Sometimes we just appreciate it. Better not to know, Even when known, better not to show. Eyes show what the mind thinks. We dip into reality, Then back to unrealistic dreams. Shrouds kept shifting, And we stood at the center: One unmoved, The other vanished, Nowhere to be seen.
- Gaurab Aryal
