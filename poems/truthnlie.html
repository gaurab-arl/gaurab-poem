Truth and Lies

A poem about the struggle between truth and deception.

Journey where I keep writing lies, Truth suffocating, hiding behind lies, Emotions were a veil covering eyes, Fog of illusion showing unrealized dreams. A wide smile stretched until corners split, Waiting to burst all organs inside mine, Living inside gutters where no one cares, Mourning until no soul is willing to bear. Saw that world no one has ever seen, A confined place, tearing flesh for meat. Left and right where death lay beside, I feared to leave everything behind.
- Gaurab Aryal
