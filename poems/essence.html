Essence Dissipating

A poem reflecting on despair, fate, and the search for peace.

Waterfall from the heavenly river, Pouring onto this crippled soul. Defiled by resentment and guilt, Swallowed by despair and sorrow. A trail to crawl, distress to feel, A meaningless journey in search of peace. Fixed fate, karma worsening, Destiny bound by anxiety’s care. Miserable one begin to praying to leave.
- Gaurab Aryal
