Innocence of Friend

Innocence you had, like a newborn, Chasing butterflies in the morning breeze. I didn’t know you were buried so deep, Searching for a way out of your own abyss. You found a face full of gleam, Chased that luster, ended up together, But along the road, you found fireflies— Tiny lights, just distractions from sight.
- Gaurab Aryal
