Care

Friend care

Let Me Burn I feel, I see, I get it— The friendship… I have it. Why do you care for me? I don’t deserve The flood of care You keep pouring into me. I misunderstood you, So deeply. The child inside you— Calling to me. The humming, The sweetness, The innocence I feel... Like a lost child Coming to me, Asking how I was— As if I were your family. But even as I hurt, Even as I burn, Don’t come and ask me. I won’t say a thing. I don’t ever want you to see The worst of my time. I will be buried with it. Don’t feel burdened— Please, live free. Don’t try to console me. I’ll break beneath your feeling. And I’ll serve you Wholeheartedly, Until you feel Annoyed By me. Don’t call me— I won’t answer it. And if I do, I won’t be able To control The turbulence My inner world is facing. I am burning With this feeling inside. Ice mountains Are melting with me. Whether I swim— Or drown— Depends on me. Don’t try To come with me. I can’t help you swim. Let me drown— And save yourself From me. Even as I suffer, I won’t tell a soul. I already know the answer. So don’t expect Me To fall.
- Gaurab Aryal
