Me

Good or bad, I can't say How to live the best way? Lost in thoughts, Didn't talk — Feels like a hard rock. Don't know what to feel or not, What I am thinking isn't about a girl — It's about my own thoughts. Looked, observed different things, Things different than today. Don't know how much we've changed — The distance between people widened, The gap is filled with greed. Now I can't sleep, Always thinking about my end. I try to be a good friend, Always ready to help — But that's all a dream. Even I don't trust me — How will others feel? The bet and promise I made, Now all is in void. The lost world, The dream I searched for. I seem to be unworthy of all. Not easy to trust. Just spend time with me — I will burst. The burst of feeling, The emotion I feel, The care I am feeling. What’s to say to boys Who don’t know themselves — Lost in the world They built for them? The difference in me and you all Is like heaven and earth. How to mix with you, If I am oil And you are water? What’s to say to girls Who hide the pain Behind the mask They wear? Can’t share with others — Is it fear or lack of trust I feel? Maybe that’s Me — Incomplete. The wall I built, Not easy to overcome. The boring person As I am — Just leaving Is the option.
- Gaurab Aryal
