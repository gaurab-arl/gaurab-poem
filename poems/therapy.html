Therapy for the Lost

Wait for Me A flow — Let things go. You don’t Have to know, Or understand Why everything comes, Then goes. Better to dodge the muddy ground And move ahead — But I’m in the middle of the ocean, No end in sight, Asking where I am, From where I lost my sight. Everywhere I see is calm, Like you — a little girl, Peaceful. But I am the turbulence, Suffering. Why so sad? It’s just me. Let go, be free. I only seem to make you Feel weary of me. So seek another me — You the beauty, I the beast — A story rewritten, Where others succeed, But I failed it. A small girl In a drum of water Feels like she’s in a pond — Drowning, suffocating, Ready to die. But I came, And saved her from that cry. Yet, She wanted to die. I know — the hidden truth, And I’m not the one You wished to be rescued by. A tsunami struck — My place. It wasn't a flood made of water, But emotion. I am just a drop of rain, Falling from the sky, While you — You are the woods, Absorbing my life. I know I’m not the best — I lack more Than you can see. You are the light, The talent. I am the shadow You tried to bury beneath. I hide my tears beneath the clouds, like rainfall. We were once together In a garden — Your care, my love — You by my side, My head in your lap. A cold wind passed — I held you close, Trying to warm you, And in your smile, In the blush, I found warmth. Even the embarrassment — I wore it Like a blanket. With tea, Your hand in mine, I felt alive. Therapy — A way to find you, Hidden from the world, Just to me. I got lost in you, Like a river slipping into a pond. Just don’t leave. Keep talking. Even if I fade — Wait for me.
- Gaurab Aryal
