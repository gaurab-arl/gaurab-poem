Dear me

"Dear Me" is a deeply emotional conversation with oneself, exploring lost hope, invisible love, and the painful realization of someone else's happiness.

Dear me, you died while finding me — the day I lost hope in everything. What were you expecting? And why are you disappearing? Did you not get anything? What's the disease I am suffering? Please fill me with the hope you feel. Things are not going my way, I am diverting. I don't feel any difficulties in the road I am walking, because I don't see the end. I am very much lacking, don't know what's fulfilling. I missed the bus I was watching. It was a bus meant for me— but it closed the door before I could reach it. Watching it leaving me, something cracked inside me. Was there something precious something priceless that I was leaving behind? A gem. It looked like a girl to me— beautiful like no other, except for the woman who raised me. I liked you very much, I accepted your bad habits. Now you just have to know me, then we will be together for eternity. Feeling hard to feel, seeing hard to see, wanting hard to get — that’s how I describe it. I am here for you, but are you there for me? You might say, "I am here," but where are you? I am searching. Maybe I will die once more while finding. I am lost to the world you are living in. Tell me about your special person — you left while talking to me. Happiness I see in your face while looking and thinking about him. I feel jealousy — how lucky is he, able to get you. How unlucky is me, not able to see you? and once you fell lacking please come to me I will hold you forever even u forget about me I will still be there, loving you — quietly, silently.
- Gaurab Aryal
