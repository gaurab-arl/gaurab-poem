Endless Beginning

Listening to music without sound, I can’t see the problem in me. I only feel distant from everything, What was once precious is now lost, And thinking of it, my heart bleeds. What could it be? Or is it within me, The lacking, the time lost too deeply? Even as I try to uplift, The hole inside is far too deep. No matter where I run, The finish line keeps increasing. At the end, I am always where I first began.
- Gaurab Aryal
