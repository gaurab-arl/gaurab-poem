Forsaken Edge

Why can’t I stop thinking? Sow a seed of clarity within me. Draw a picture staring back at me A deep gaze, the forsaken me. No matter what changes, I am still me. No matter how I feel, A messy mind, An exhausted body, Eyes dried, Numb with everything, Unable to touch happiness. Even illusions feel fake. Survival was only a game. I walk a path already laid, Standing at the edge, Jumping at the sight of fright.
- Gaurab Aryal
