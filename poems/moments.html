Moments

Flooded Silence The moments I wanted to spend with you all — They burn within me Like a volcano Waiting to erupt. But… The fear whispers: “You will be left alone In the search for them.” I have a child within me, too — Curious, Craving to explore. But chains bind it. Each moment I try to create. Create a dead end for me How do you solve it The demons in me Remind me, “You never deserved Those things.” I try to pray to God, To ask for forgiveness… But I stumble, Again and again. Maybe He thinks I’m just Wasting His time. I long for something— Something to spark me, To begin a new journey. But finding such a soul Feels like Rain in a desert. Always dry. Even when I want to cry, My eyes remain closed — Tears trapped inside. And now, I am filled with flood. When I open… I flood with it And take everything With me.
- Gaurab Aryal
